Job Title: Data Analyst

Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and interpret data sets.
- Develop dashboards and reports using Power BI or Tableau.
- Identify trends and patterns in complex datasets.
- Communicate insights with stakeholders.

Required Skills:
- SQL, Excel, Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data visualization (Tableau, Power BI)
- Statistical analysis, Data cleaning
- Business communication
